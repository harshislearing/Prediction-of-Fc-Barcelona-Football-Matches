ABOUT CLUB

FC Barcelona, founded in 1899, is one of the most prestigious football clubs in the world, known for its attacking philosophy, rich history, and commitment to developing young talent through its famed La Masia academy. Based in Barcelona, Spain, the club competes in La Liga and has won multiple domestic and international titles, including numerous La Liga and UEFA Champions League trophies. The team plays its home matches at the iconic Spotify Camp Nou. FC Barcelona is also known for its historic rivalry with Real Madrid, known as El Clásico. With a strong global fanbase, the club operates under the motto "Més que un club" (More than a club), reflecting its deep cultural and social influence beyond football.

HISTORY

FC Barcelona was founded in 1899 by a group of Swiss, English, and Catalan footballers led by Joan Gamper. The club has grown to become a symbol of Catalan culture and identity. Over the years, Barça has won numerous domestic and international titles, including 26 La Liga titles and 5 UEFA Champions League trophies.

CAMP NOU

Camp Nou, located in Barcelona, is the home stadium of FC Barcelona. With a seating capacity of over 99,000, it is the largest stadium in Europe. The stadium has hosted numerous historic matches, including the 1982 FIFA World Cup final and the 1992 Summer Olympics football final.
